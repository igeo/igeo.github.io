What's Expected of Us

我们被期待做的事

Original story by Ted Chiang

原著：特德·姜

This is a warning. By now you’ve probably seen a Predictor. It’s a small device, like a remote for opening your car door. Its only features are a button and a big green LED.

这是一个警告。到现在为止，你可能已经见过“预测器”了。它是一个小设备，就像你的汽车遥控钥匙。它唯一的特征是一个按钮和一个巨大的绿色LED灯。

The light flashes one second before you press the button. If you try to break the rules, you find that you can’t. There’s no way to fool a Predictor.

灯光会在你按下按钮的一秒钟之前闪烁。如果你试图打破规则，你会发现你做不到。没有任何方法可以欺骗预测器。

Predictors demonstrate that there’s no such thing as free will. The experience is too powerful for an argument to overrule.

预测器证明了自由意志并不存在。这种体验太过强烈，任何论证都无法推翻它。

Typically, a person plays with a Predictor compulsively for several days, showing it to friends, trying various schemes to outwit the device.

通常，一个人会强迫性地玩预测器好几天，向朋友展示，尝试各种计谋来胜过这个设备。

Eventually, a third of those who play with a Predictor must be hospitalized. The end state is akinetic mutism, a kind of waking coma. The motivation to move is gone.

最终，玩预测器的人中有三分之一必须住院。最终状态是“无动性缄默症”，一种清醒的昏迷。移动的动力消失了。

Doctors argue: "You had no free will before, why change now?" The patients invariably respond: "But now I know." And some of them never say anything again.

医生争辩说：“你以前也没有自由意志，为什么要现在改变？”病人总是回答：“但现在我知道了。”他们中的一些人再也没说过一句话。

I’m transmitting this warning from a year in your future. My message is this: pretend that you have free will. Civilization now depends on self-deception.

我是从你未来的一年后发来这个警告的。我的信息是：假装你有自由意志。文明现在依赖于自欺欺人。

I know it’s all predetermined who will succumb. My warning won’t alter those proportions. So why did I do it? Because I had no choice.

我知道谁会屈服都是注定的。我的警告不会改变这些比例。那我为什么还要这么做？因为我别无选择。
